`npmCli.ts` only: `/\bnpx\s+(--yes|-y)\b/.test(install.cmd)` → `console.warn('ephemeral install: nothing to uninstall...')` + exit 0. Other 6 uninstallers not ephemeral.

### D-05/D-06/D-07 in cli/uninstall.ts
- No `--apply` → `[dry-run] would uninstall '<name>' via method '<method>'` + exit 2
- `--yes` without `--apply` → `error: --yes requires --apply` + exit 2 (H1 gate)
- `--apply` without `--yes` → `p.confirm()` default No; cancel → exit 2
- No `keepBackup` field in RawOpts (D-07 explicit omission)
